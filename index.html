About Me

Hi, I am a 4th year PhD Student at the University of Texas at Austin advised by Prof Raghu Bollapragada focusing on designing Nonlinear Optimization Algorithms. We delve into a diverse array of domains, including subjects like stochastic, constrained, and decentralized optimization. My primary emphasis revolves around designing problem-specific algorithms that align with the primary costs of the given system. I am consistently open to engaging in discussions and potential collaborative on various projects.
